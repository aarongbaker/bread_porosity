# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of behavior that creates a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping and supporting fellow contributors

### Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations
- Removing, editing, or rejecting content that violates this Code
- Removing comment privileges or banning contributors for serious violations

### Reporting

**To report a violation:**

1. **Email**: Report serious violations directly to the project maintainers
2. **GitHub**: Use Issue or private message for less severe violations
3. **Anonymous**: Information can be reported confidentially

Include:
- Description of the incident
- Names of involved parties (if applicable)
- Impact of the behavior
- Any relevant context or evidence

All reports will be:
- Reviewed promptly and thoroughly
- Handled confidentially (except when required by law)
- Investigated fairly and impartially
- Treated seriously

### Response

Responses may include:
- Warnings or requests to cease behavior
- Temporary suspension of participation
- Permanent removal from the community
- Other appropriate actions

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Project-related events and gatherings

---

## Our Responsibility

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct consistently
- Protecting reporter confidentiality
- Providing a safe environment for all contributors
- Welcoming and supporting new contributors
